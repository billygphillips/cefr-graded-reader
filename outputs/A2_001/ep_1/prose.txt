The house is dusty. Marco opens the front door. The air smells old. He walks into the living room. Dust is on every surface.

Mr. Webb is in the kitchen. He is cleaning the counters.

"You start upstairs," Mr. Webb says. "Work fast. We have a lot to do."

Marco nods. He walks up the stairs. His eyes move quickly. He looks at every object. A broken clock. An old picture frame. A toy car on the floor. He wants to know the stories behind these things.

He opens a door at the end of the hallway. This room is different. It is a child's room. A small bed is in the corner. A wardrobe is open. Marco looks inside. Nothing is there.

He walks to another room. This room has a broken cabinet. The doors are hanging off. Marco opens the cabinet. Inside, there is an old video camera.

He picks it up. It is heavy. He looks at the screen on the back. It is cracked. He opens a small door on the side. A tape is inside.

Marco takes the tape out. He holds it in his hand. It is small and black. He puts it in his pocket.

He walks downstairs. He looks into the living room. An old TV is against the wall. He checks the back. There is a wire. He takes the camera and the wire. He walks to the TV.

He connects the wire. He turns on the TV. The screen is grey. He puts the tape into the camera. He connects the camera to the TV.

A picture appears on the screen.

A man is standing in a room. A woman is next to him. A young girl is behind them. Their faces look frightened. The man looks at the camera. He does not speak.

The man bends down. He lifts a floorboard. A metal box is under the floorboard. He takes the box. He puts it back. He fixes the floorboard.

The woman walks to the wall. She takes a pencil. She writes something. Then she stops. She wipes the writing off with her hand.

The picture stops. The screen is grey again.

Marco sits on the floor. He thinks about what he saw. The family was frightened. They were hiding something. The box is under the floorboard.

He takes his pencil from his pocket. He walks to the spot on the floor. He makes a small mark on the wood. Now he knows where to look.

He takes the camera and the tape. He puts them in his bag. He finishes his work in the house. He cleans the rooms. He empties the bins.

Mr. Webb waits at the front door.

"Done?" he asks.

"Yes," Marco says.

They leave the house. Mr. Webb locks the door.

Marco walks home. He cannot stop thinking about the box. Who was in the video? What was inside the box? Why were they so frightened?

He must come back next weekend. He must find out what is under the floorboard.
